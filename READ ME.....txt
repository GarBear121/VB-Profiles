Farming profiles for 2018 and lower are all named and will not be changed.

Farming profiles for 2019 and beyond are number. They correspond with the level of the area.

Example... Un'Goro (2019) is ~ 49-52. Un'Goro 1 (2019) is ~ 51-55. And so on

Death and Vendor waypoints are added as needed.

Any farming profile with a '0' ( example: WPL 01 (2019) or Un'Goro 03 (2019) ) are not finished!
They do not work properly or you will die often!



Combat profiles for 2019 and beyond have INSTRUCTIONS at the top of each profile!
